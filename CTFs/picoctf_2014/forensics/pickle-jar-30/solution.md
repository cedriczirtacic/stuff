## Solution
JAR format is based on ZIP archive format.
```bash
$ unzip pickle.jar
Archive:  pickle.jar
   creating: com/
   creating: com/picoctf/
  inflating: com/picoctf/Jar.class
   creating: META-INF/
  inflating: META-INF/MANIFEST.MF
 extracting: pickle.p
$ cat pickle.p
S'YOUSTOLETHEPICKLES'
p0
.
```
